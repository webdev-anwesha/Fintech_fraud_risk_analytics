# Fintech_fraud_risk_analytics
End-to-end FinTech Risk &amp; Fraud Analytics Platform built with PostgreSQL, Python, Power BI, and SQL to detect fraudulent transactions, assess customer risk, and deliver business insights through interactive dashboards.
# Business Problem Statement

Financial fraud is one of the biggest challenges faced by fintech companies, resulting in significant monetary losses, operational inefficiencies, regulatory risks, and reduced customer trust. As transaction volumes grow, traditional rule-based monitoring systems become increasingly ineffective at identifying sophisticated fraud patterns in real time.

The organization currently lacks a centralized analytics platform to monitor fraudulent activities, identify high-risk customers and merchants, evaluate geographic and transactional risk, and provide actionable insights for business stakeholders. Fraud investigators spend considerable time manually analyzing transaction records, making it difficult to respond quickly to emerging threats and prioritize high-risk cases effectively.

To address these challenges, this project aims to design and develop an end-to-end FinTech Risk & Fraud Analytics Platform that integrates data engineering, SQL analytics, Python-based data processing, and interactive business intelligence dashboards. The platform enables fraud analysts, risk management teams, and business executives to monitor fraud trends, calculate customer and merchant risk scores, identify suspicious transaction patterns, and support data-driven decision-making for fraud prevention.

By transforming raw transactional data into meaningful business insights, the platform demonstrates how modern fintech organizations leverage analytics to reduce fraud losses, improve operational efficiency, strengthen risk management strategies, and build customer trust.
